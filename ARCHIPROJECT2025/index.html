Welcome to JDM FEST 2025

Get ready for the most exciting summer fest of the year, where you can witness the ultimate showcase of JDM cars in action on our grand track course!

Featured Cars Last Year

Nissan Skyline GT-R

Ever since I was a kid, I dreamed of owning a Skyline GT-R. Now, after years of searching, saving, and wrenching late into the night, I’m finally bringing mine back to life. Every scratch, every part I replace—it’s all part of the journey. This isn’t just a car to me. It’s a piece of my past, my passion, and a reminder that some dreams are worth chasing no matter how long it takes.

Toyota Supra

Ever since I first heard the roar of a Supra, I knew I had to have one. It took time, patience, and a whole lot of grit, but now I have managed to restore mine piece by piece. Every turn of the wrench brings me closer to the car I’ve always imagined—raw, powerful, and mine. It’s more than just a build. It’s a connection to the past, a tribute to JDM legends, and a promise I made to myself years ago: never stop chasing what sets your soul on fire.

Mazda RX-7

The RX-7 has always had a special place in my heart— something about that rotary engine and the way it handles just hits different. Restoring mine isn’t just about bringing back a classic, it’s about rebuilding a piece of my own story. Every panel, every tune, every long night in the garage is part of the journey. This isn’t just a car it’s a dream I refused to let go of, and now, I can bring my dream everywhere i go.
